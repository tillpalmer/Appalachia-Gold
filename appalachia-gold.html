Our Philosophy

How We Work

From seed to extract

Step One
Mountain Cultivation

Single-source genetics grown in living soil at elevation. Cold nights and mineral-rich water stress the plant into producing dense, terpene-laden flowers.

Step Two
Selective Harvest

Hand-harvested at peak cannabinoid and terpene expression. Flash-frozen within hours for live products, or slow-dried and cured for weeks in our mountain facility.

Step Three
Low-Temp Ethanol Extraction

All products are extracted using sub-zero ethanol — cold enough to preserve every terpene and minor cannabinoid the plant produced, without pulling harsh chlorophylls or lipids. Slow, deliberate, and impossibly clean.

Step Four
Lab Verification

Every batch is third-party tested for potency, terpene profile, pesticides, heavy metals, and microbials before it leaves our facility. COAs published openly.
